Conclusion

Based on my research, I concluded that danceability does not affect popularity (in the top 50 songs) and pop genres are the most popular genre. Additionally, there is a wide variety of genres and artists in the top 50; the artist with the most songs in top 50 (Ed Sheernan) only amounts to 4 of the songs. This suggests users of Spotify are very diverse, and more research could be done using music statistics from spotify as music is an interesting demographic that may correlate to different backgrounds.
